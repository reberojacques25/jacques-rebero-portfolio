Context & Problem
Inyange Industries experienced a decline in juice sales between 2023 and 2024 despite stable pricing and production. Management needed data-driven insights to identify underperforming products and branches.

Objective

  • Analyze sales trends across time and locations
  • Identify underperforming juice flavors
  • Assess the impact of stockouts on revenue

Data

  • Weekly and daily sales by branch
  • Sales by juice flavor
  • Stockout frequency
  • Revenue contribution per product

Tools & Methods

  • Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Trend analysis, comparison analysis, heatmaps

Key Insights

  • Overall sales declined in 2024, especially in Q2 and Q3
  • Mango and Pineapple flavors underperformed
  • Nyabugogo and Kimironko branches had high stockout rates
  • Weekend sales consistently outperformed weekdays

Recommendations

  • Improve inventory management in high-risk branches
  • Reevaluate or reposition low-performing flavors
  • Introduce weekend promotions and bundled offers
  • Apply time series forecasting for demand planning
